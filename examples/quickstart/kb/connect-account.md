# Connecting acme-app to your account

<public>
To connect acme-app to your account, open Settings → Account → Connect, then
sign in with the same email you used to sign up. The connection completes once
you see the green check next to your account name.
</public>

<internal>
If the connect button does nothing, it's almost always a stale session token —
have them sign out and back in.
</internal>
